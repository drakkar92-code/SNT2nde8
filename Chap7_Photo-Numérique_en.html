Digital photos

Digital photos are images captured, stored, and displayed as digital files. They have revolutionized traditional photography by simplifying the process of capturing, processing, and sharing images.

  • Digital photos are images captured and stored as digital files using devices equipped with sensors, such as cameras, smartphones, or drones. They offer a resolution expressed in pixels, a wide variety of file formats (JPEG, RAW, PNG, etc.), and varying color depths for precise rendering. They allow for instant visualization, easy storage, rapid sharing, and advanced editing through dedicated software. However, they can suffer from quality loss with compression, be vulnerable to data loss, and depend on technological compatibility. Digital photography is constantly evolving thanks to advancements such as computational photography, the integration of artificial intelligence, increased resolution, and the emergence of immersive formats like 360° photos. This technology has transformed the way images are created, shared, and used in our daily lives.
